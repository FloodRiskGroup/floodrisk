FloodRisk
*********
.. topic::  What is FloodRisk ?

    **FloodRisk** is a *QGIS plugin*  that provides the assessment of flood consequences,
    in terms of loss of life and direct economic damages.

.. topic:: The term RISK  :  Risk = (Probability) x (Consequences)
